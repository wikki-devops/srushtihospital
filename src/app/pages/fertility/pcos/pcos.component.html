Case Study: Roopa's Journey - Beyond Fertility

Background

Roopa, a 27-year-old software engineer, came to Srushti Fertility Centre seeking help for her persistent health issues rather than fertility concerns. She had been experiencing severe acne, weight gain, and irregular menstrual cycles for years, which she attributed to stress and her sedentary lifestyle.

Symptoms and Struggles:

  • Acne and Skin Issues: Roopa struggled with acne that affected her self-esteem.
  • Weight Gain: Despite her efforts to maintain a healthy diet, she found it challenging to lose weight.
  • Irregular Periods: Her menstrual cycles were unpredictable, leading to anxiety and frustration.

Main Causes of PCOS:

Through consultations with Dr. Samundi Sankari and Dr. Divya Sivaraman, Roopa learned that her symptoms were linked to PCOS, a hormonal disorder affecting many women, especially those with sedentary lifestyles.

Lifestyle Modifications and Professional Care

Dietary Changes

Roopa was guided to adopt a balanced diet rich in whole foods and low in processed sugars.

Exercise Regimen

A personalized exercise plan was created, incorporating both cardio and strength training.

Stress Management

Mindfulness and yoga sessions were introduced to help manage stress.

Outcome

After six months of dedicated effort and professional guidance, Roopa experienced significant improvements in her skin, weight, and menstrual regularity. She felt empowered and in control of her health.

"When I was diagnosed with PCOS, I felt lost and overwhelmed. I remember looking in the mirror and not recognizing the person staring back at me. But through support and education, I learned to embrace my journey. I started exercising and eating healthier, and slowly, I began to feel like myself again. To every woman out there struggling with PCOS, remember: you are not alone. Take that first step, no matter how small, and reclaim your life. You are stronger than you think. Taking charge of my health was the best decision I ever made. I feel like a new person! - Roopa
