About Srushti

Babies are proof that miracles happen.

Every couple dreams of creating its own miracle, an adorable bundle of joy. Many a times though, the journey to parenthood can be difficult. At Srushti, we understand how important the desire to have a baby is. Since 1993, we have been successfully helping couples work through their fertility issues to finally realise their dream of becoming parents. It is our mission to provide you with assistance every step of the way with the most advanced services in fertility treatment. The serene atmosphere at Srushti coupled with our extremely supportive staff will make your experience memorable.

For over 20 years, Srushti has been a trusted fertility center in Chennai, led by IVF pioneer Dr. S. Samundi Sankari. As Founder and Medical Director, she also leads the Srushti Medical Research Foundation, tackling infertility and women's health issues. Dr. Sankari has helped countless couples achieve parenthood and is globally recognized, earning several honors, including the Best Distinguishing Physician award in 2001.
