What Drives Us And How We Achieve It

Our Vision

We aim to provide reliable, high quality, personalized care for patients across the globe, to treat fertility-related conditions and other areas of women’s care, by adopting advanced technologies, and thereby be a one stop solution centre for women.

At Srushti, we believe that combining surgical intervention and Assisted Reproductive techniques, it improves your chances of getting pregnant significantly, particularly in cases of minimal to mild endometriosis, presence of adhesion, tubal blocks and ovarian cysts. - Dr. Samundi Sankari.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide individualized and compassionate care through comprehensive and competent services.

Green IVF or Mini IVF offers a number of benefits to couples trying to get pregnant, aside from being less expensive. This includes the elimination of the risk of Ovarian Hyper- Stimulation Syndrome (OHSS). It is especially suited for women who are prone to hyper-stimulation. - Dr. Divya Sivaraman.
