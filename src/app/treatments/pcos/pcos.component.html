What is PCOS?

Polycystic Ovary Syndrome(PCOS) is a common hormonal condition affecting women of reproductive age. It can cause irregular periods, excess male hormones (androgens) and multiple small cysts in the ovaries. Often diagnosed in early adulthood, PCOS may impact fertility, metabolism, and overall health but with the right care it can be managed effectively.
